Magnesium Glycinate
300–400mg elemental/night
 Magnesium Bisglycinate — bulk.com ~£4/month Confirmed bisglycinate form ✓ — the only form worth buying. Magnesium oxide (the most common cheap form) has ~4% absorption. H&B's own-brand magnesium glycinate costs ~£30/month for the same dose — bulk.com is 7× cheaper.
