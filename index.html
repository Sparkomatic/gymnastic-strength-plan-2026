What "toning" actually means

There is no such thing as toning a muscle — muscles grow or they shrink. What people call "toned" is simply having enough muscle mass that it's visible under a low enough body fat percentage. That means two things working together: resistance training to build and preserve muscle, and a modest calorie deficit to reduce fat.

Emma's plan is built on this principle. Three to four days of progressive resistance training will build lean muscle. Nutrition creates a gentle deficit without sacrificing protein — ensuring muscle stays while fat reduces. Cardio is a useful tool but not the primary driver.
